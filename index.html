My Personal Experience at AkiraChix

During my first week at AkiraChix, I was really surprised by the diversity and new environment. I found my self taking many pictures aroudnd the campus. However, I also experienced a lot of confusion, particularly with the language and the food. My struggle with English was compounded by the different accents I encountered, which were unlike what I had learned in high school. Additionaly, I was perplexed by the new foods that were served, many of which I had never seen before. Fortunately, just three days later, I grew to really like all the food.
